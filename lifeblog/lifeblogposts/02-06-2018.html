Church, Super Bowl LII, and a Cold Winter

by EveryDayKenneth

Posted on Tuesday, February 06, 2018

A Real Winter

Church on Sunday morning was pretty average, but after the sermon, the pastor prayed for me; that same night, I ended up having my very first lucid dream where I actually knew that I was in a dream. I’m beginning to believe again in the power of prayer.

Yesterday was an exciting Monday – I got to watch the Super Bowl and the game was absolutely fantastic. However, the weather outside was 8 degrees Celsius in Taipei and raining. I was asked to chair Monday evening’s meeting at the hospital, but only John showed up – it must be due to the weather.

Lord, make me an instrument of your peace. Where there is hatred, let me bring love. Where there is offense, let me bring pardon. Where there is discord, let me bring union. Where there is error, let me bring truth. Where there is doubt, let me bring faith. Where there is despair, let me bring hope. Where there is darkness, let me bring your light. Where there is sadness, let me bring joy. O Master, let me not seek as much to be consoled as to console, to be understood as to understand, to be loved as to love, for it is in giving that one receives, it is in self-forgetting that one finds, it is in pardoning that one is pardoned, it is in dying that one is raised to eternal life.

Prayer of St. Francis

Right now, Robbie and I are plowing through questions regarding our company and ironing out the contracts between us – we have one of the largest law firms in Taiwan consulting us as we begin to register the company. These are exciting times for us and I hope we can continue to “create a dent in the universe” with our work.
