1964-1965 Los Angeles Lakers Review

by EveryDayKenneth

Posted on November 22, 2017

It’s becoming so frustrating using these old school classic all-time teams: Jerry West and Elgin Baylor can play very well – Rudy Larusso will grab his fair share of boards, but it’s really a difficult task to play well with these older all-time teams.

Don Nelson gets his fair share of boards as well, but this team is undersized to match up with modern day teams such as the Oklahoma City Thunder or Cleveland Cavaliers. Elgin Baylor has an awkward shot release for the corner 3 – Jerry West makes a lot of mid range jumpers.

I’m beginning to think that maybe I should only start taking 2 point shots because a small percentage of my 3s go in. When using this team online, I lose by A LOT – and playing 2K just isn’t fun anymore (especially when you’re on the wrong side of a blowout game).

You can't get much done in life if you only work on the days when you feel good.

Jerry West 1965 Los Angeles Lakers
